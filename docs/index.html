⟶ Three principles

Built to stay out of your way.

— 01

Reads the whole conversation

AI gets the full email thread as context — not just the last message. It sees what's been said, who said it, and what needs a response.

Use any model you want

— 03

Your keys. Your data.

No accounts, no subscriptions, no servers in the middle. API keys live in the macOS Keychain. Your email never touches our infrastructure — because there isn't any.
